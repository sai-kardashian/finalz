  • Click Start Button to begin!
  • Use arrow keys to control the direction of the snake
  • Guide the snake to eat the red food squares to grow and increase your score.
  • Don't let the snake hit the walls or itself. If it does, the game ends.
  • After the game ends, you can click "Start Game" again to play.
  • Watch for rules: Every 10 seconds, a new rule will be applied changing how the game behaves.
  • Speed Up!: The snake moves faster.
  • Inverted Controls!: Up is down, left is right, etc.
  • Rotated Controls!: Your directional inputs are rotated 90 degrees clockwise (e.g., pressing Up moves Right).
  • Bombs Appear!: A purple bomb will appear. Eating it will halve your snake's length and reduce your score.
  • Enjoy the chaotic fun of Snake Game Roulette!
